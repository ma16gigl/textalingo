Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2025

Welcome to TextALingo, a language learning platform designed to help you explore stories in various languages, including endangered ones. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and user ID via Supabase authentication.
  • Payment Information: For premium subscriptions, we use Stripe to process payments. We collect payment details (e.g., card information) indirectly through Stripe, but we do not store this data ourselves.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data on how you interact with stories, languages selected, and saved vocabulary words to improve your experience.
  • Support Contributions: A portion of premium subscription fees supports 7000.org for endangered language preservation—we don’t collect additional data for this.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Provide and personalize your learning experience (e.g., story access, vocabulary tools).
  • Process premium subscriptions securely via Stripe.
  • Support endangered language initiatives through 7000.org.
  • Improve our app based on usage patterns.

3. Data Sharing

We share your data only with:

  • Stripe: For payment processing (see Stripe’s Privacy Policy).
  • Supabase: For authentication and data storage (see Supabase’s Privacy Policy).
  • 7000.org: Aggregated, anonymized contribution data for language preservation.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure. Contact us if you suspect a breach.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access or delete your account data via your profile.
  • Cancel your premium subscription anytime through Stripe.
  • Email us at [textalingo@gmail.com] for support or data requests.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy—check back here for changes.

7. Contact Us

For questions, email [textalingo@gmail.com].
